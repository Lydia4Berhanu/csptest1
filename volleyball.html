Ever since our season came to an end I've been trying my hardest to put in extra work during the off season. I do daily workouts and play select volleyball. Select Volleyball also known as Club volleyball is kind of like school teams,the different teams are divided by age. For example I play on a U15 team for Northshore Juniors. The age is determined by the age you are turning that school year, I am currently 14 years old but after January 18 I will be 15.
